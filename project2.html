Hero Born Custom

View on GitHub

Hero Born Custom operates as a third-person action-adventure title which requires players to gather coins for victory while fighting enemies through projectile-based combat. The game expands upon the original Hero Born project through its custom art and destructible enemies and dynamic minimap and advanced camera controls and post-processing effects.

The original Hero Born project, based on Learning C# by Developing Games with Unity, introduces fundamental gameplay systems including basic player movement, a third-person camera, projectile shooting mechanics, simple enemy AI using NavMesh patrols, item pickups, health tracking, and scene management.

Hero Born "Custom" expands upon the original by including the follwing features:

  • Custom enemy models
  • Minimap with render texture system
  • Advanced camera controls with rotation and zoom
  • Custom item pickups granting special abilities
  • Post-processing visual effects
  • Dynamic enemy destruction on hit
  • Projectile-based shooting system improvements
  • Pause menu with restart, quit, and volume controls
  • Background music and sound effect integration
  • Win condition based on coin collection
